Purpose

The Analyst Archive is the central repository for managing all drone, camera, and radar detection reports. Add, edit, and delete reports as new information becomes available.

Creating Reports

Click "+ New Report" to add a detection. Provide coordinates (latitude/longitude), altitude, and a description of the detection.

Editing Reports

Click "Edit" on any report in the table to update its information. Changes are saved immediately to the backend.

Deleting Reports

Click "Delete" to remove a report. You will be asked to confirm before the report is permanently deleted.

Backend Integration

All reports are stored in a backend system where authority users can access them for corridor violation analysis and alerting. Reports can also be shared with colleagues via JSON export.
